About Ian DePriest

Born in Singapore and raised in Kansas, Ian DePriest is a voice actor and singer songwriter currently based in Nashville, Tennessee. At a young age he experienced many cultures and saw how widely different yet similar people can be even when theyâ€™re worlds apart.

From listening to folktales in the midwest to the myths of his own cultural roots across the globe; there was always heart in the stories people told. And that is how he fell in love with storytelling. Whether it is to escape into fantastical places, or to be grounded in the contradictions of being human, Ian seeks to bring people and their hearts together through his acting and music. Thank you for visiting and we hope these stories bring you some comfort.
